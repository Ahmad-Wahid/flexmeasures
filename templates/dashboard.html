About

The Balancing Valorisation Platform (BVP) is a tool for scheduling balancing actions on behalf of connected asset owners. Its purpose is to realise the best value for the owners by selling balancing services to energy markets. It fulfills this purpose with four services: automation, insight, autonomy, and profit sharing.

This dashboard shows you which of your assets are connected to the platform and how they are doing. Assets marked red require your attention, as balancing opportunities have been identified.
