About

This is a Virtual Power Plant (VPP) platform for Aggregators and Energy Service Companies operating on Jeju island. The VPP consists of many resources located on the island, whose electrical load are monitored and forecasted by the platform. The platform also monitors and forecasts market prices for electricity. Finally, the platform offers various control options over Active Demand & Supply from flexible resources within the platform.
